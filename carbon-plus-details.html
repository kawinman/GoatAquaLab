Carbon+ Algae Prevention & Organic Carbon Supplement is an algae prevention product for all types of planted aquariums. Daily use inhibits invisible algae spores within the system, addressing the problem at its source and preventing Black Brush Algae (BBA), Hair Algae, and Staghorn Algae from starting to grow. It also provides a supplemental source of organic carbon that plants can utilize as reserve energy, helping them grow stronger and better compete against algae. Carbon+ is the key to a truly balanced and beautiful ecosystem.
